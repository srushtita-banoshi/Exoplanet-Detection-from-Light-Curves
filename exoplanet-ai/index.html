Explainable AI

See why the model flags a signal as an exoplanet. No black box.

1. Transit depth

Brightness dip ∝ (R_planet/R_star)². We measure depth in ppm.

2. Periodicity

Real planets cause repeating dips. We search for the best period.

3. Shape & noise

Transit shape and stellar noise are used to validate the signal.

Glossary

Transit
Planet passing in front of its star; causes a brightness dip.
Light curve
Brightness vs. time; periodic dips suggest a planet.
R⊕
Earth radii; 1 R⊕ ≈ 6,371 km.
