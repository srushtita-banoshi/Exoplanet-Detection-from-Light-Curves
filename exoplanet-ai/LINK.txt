Exoplanet AI — Your link
========================

Open in browser (when server is running):

  http://127.0.0.1:8080

To start the server: double-click RUN.bat in the exoplanet-ai folder,
or run:  python -m http.server 8080 --bind 127.0.0.1
(from inside the exoplanet-ai folder)

To open without server: open index.html directly in your browser.
